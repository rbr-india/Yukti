About Yukti

Decades of Expertise.

Our Story

Yukti Trading Pte Ltd is a Singapore-incorporated real estate advisory firm established to serve institutional and private capital deploying into global real estate markets.

Yukti was founded on a simple observation: the best real estate advice and the biggest commercial incentives rarely sit in the same room. We built a firm where they never have to.

The Yukti team brings together over 40 years of real estate experience spanning advisory, deal origination, market research, and portfolio strategy across Asia-Pacific, the Middle East, Europe, the Americas, and South Asia. Our advisory work is grounded in direct market exposure — our advisors have been involved in transactions, fund structures, and investment analysis across more than fifteen markets worldwide.

The firm operates from Singapore, one of the world's foremost financial centres, leveraging the city-state's position as the hub connecting Asian and Middle Eastern capital with property markets globally.

Yukti works with a select group of institutional clients and family capital groups on a retainer and project basis. We do not manage funds, hold brokerage licences, or take principal positions — our independence is the foundation of the trust our clients place in us.

Independence

No brokerage. No fund management. No co-investment interests. Yukti's advice is free of the conflicts that compromise most advisory relationships in real estate.

Precision

We deal in facts, data, and rigorous analysis — built on primary research, direct market intelligence, and decades of deal experience. Not recycled commentary.

Discretion

We work quietly. Client relationships, mandates, and transaction details are held in strict confidence. Our clients value this as much as our analytical capability.

Long-Term Partnership

Our engagements are structured around enduring advisory relationships, not transaction-driven incentives. Our success is measured by the quality of our clients' outcomes.

1. Introduction

Yukti Trading Pte Ltd (UEN 202517859W), incorporated in Singapore ("Yukti", "we", "our"), is committed to protecting the privacy of all individuals who interact with us. This Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal data in accordance with Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA) and, where applicable, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

By submitting an enquiry, engaging our services, or otherwise communicating with us, you consent to the collection and use of your personal data as described in this Policy.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the personal data necessary for the purpose for which it is collected. This may include:

  • Identity and contact data: name, title, company name, email address, telephone number.
  • Engagement data: details of your enquiry, mandate, or advisory engagement, including information about your investment objectives, portfolio, and transaction interests.
  • Communication data: records of correspondence between you and Yukti.
  • Website data: standard technical data collected when you visit our website, including browser type, device type, and pages visited. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics tools.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and assess whether an advisory relationship is appropriate;
  • Perform our advisory services under any engagement or Master Services Agreement;
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations;
  • Maintain records for audit and compliance purposes.

We do not use your personal data for marketing, profiling, or automated decision-making. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party.

4. Disclosure of Data

Your personal data is shared on a strict need-to-know basis within the Yukti team. We may disclose your data to third parties only in the following circumstances:

  • Where required by applicable law, regulation, court order, or governmental authority — in which case we will, to the extent legally permitted, notify you before disclosure;
  • With professional advisers (legal, accounting, or compliance) bound by confidentiality obligations;
  • Where you have expressly authorised such disclosure in writing.

We do not transfer personal data outside Singapore or the EEA without ensuring adequate safeguards are in place as required by applicable law.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. For active client relationships, data is retained for the duration of the engagement and for seven (7) years thereafter for legal and compliance purposes. Enquiry data from non-clients is retained for no longer than two (2) years from the date of last contact.

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, alteration, or destruction. Access to personal data within Yukti is restricted to team members with a legitimate need. All client engagement data is handled with the same level of discretion as confidential information under our service agreements.

7. Your Rights

Under the PDPA and, where applicable, the GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, withdraw consent to, or request erasure of your personal data held by us, subject to applicable exceptions. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at sales@yukti.sg. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

Our website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics tools. No personal data is collected automatically when you visit the site beyond standard server log data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The current version and its effective date will always be published on our website. Material changes will be communicated directly to active clients.
